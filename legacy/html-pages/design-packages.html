Pricing FAQs

How do you charge for your services?

Our pricing is project-based and depends on scope, complexity, and timeline. After an initial consultation, I provide a detailed proposal outlining all costs. No hidden fees or surprises.

What's not included in the design fee?

Design fees cover my professional services (design, planning, project management). Separate costs include furniture, materials, contractor labor, and any product purchases. I help you establish and manage a realistic budget for these items.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes. I require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange a payment schedule tied to project milestones.

Can I use my own contractors?

Absolutely! I'm happy to work with contractors you trust. I can also recommend qualified professionals from my network of Santa Fe contractors, all vetted for quality and reliability.

What if my project budget changes?

We'll establish a budget during our initial consultation and I'll design within those parameters. If your budget changes, we can adjust the scope accordingly. My goal is to maximize value at any price point.

Do you offer payment plans?

For larger projects, I can create a custom payment schedule that aligns with project phases. Contact me to discuss options that work for your situation.
