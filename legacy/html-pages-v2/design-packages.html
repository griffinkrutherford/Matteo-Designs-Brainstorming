Frequently Asked Questions

How do you charge for your services?

Our pricing is project-based and depends on scope, complexity, and timeline. After an initial consultation, we provide a detailed proposal outlining all costs. No hidden fees.

What's not included in the design fee?

Design fees cover our professional services. Separate costs include furniture, materials, contractor labor, and product purchases. We help establish and manage a realistic budget for these items.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes. We require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange a payment schedule tied to milestones.

Can I use my own contractors?

Absolutely. We work with contractors you trust, or we can recommend qualified professionals from our network.

What if my budget changes?

We'll establish a budget during our initial consultation and design within those parameters. If your budget changes, we can adjust the scope accordingly.
