About Mattéo Designs

Who We Are

Mattéo Designs has been in business for over 25 years. Our interiors are well suited to the urban lifestyle and modern design aesthetics of today. We focus on construction methodologies that marry traditional glamour with cutting edge design.

With projects located throughout the United States, we specialize in creating livable spaces that bring our clients’ vision to life from concept to completion.

What We Do

We work in both residential and commercial design. Whether it be a restaurant, a boutique hotel, a primary residence or a vacation home, we do it all.

Our services include: Design Development, Space Planning, Lighting Design, Material Selection, FF&E (Furniture, Fixtures & Equipment) Selection, and Project Management.

Our Philosophy

We believe that Interior Design can change lives and that there is nothing more transformative than an artfully rendered space.

We focus on functional solutions to design challenges and we create interior spaces that have a powerful impact on the way our clients live their daily lives.

Mattéo Designs interiors are well suited to the urban lifestyle that has become so intrinsic to the Interior Design landscape of today.
