Who We Are

Decades of experience, reimagined for Santa Fe.

Mattéo Bitetti began his interior design career in 1998, transforming New York City lofts with cutting-edge design and traditional craftsmanship. Since founding Mattéo Designs in 2022, he brings this expertise to Santa Fe and beyond, delivering comprehensive design services from concept through completion.

What We Do

Full-service residential and commercial design for Santa Fe restaurants, boutique hotels, primary residences, and destination vacation homes. Services span design development, space planning, material selection, custom furniture, architectural drawings, and project management—delivered on-site or via Zoom-first collaboration with architects and builders worldwide.

Our Philosophy

We believe materials can change lives. Natural plasters, hand-rubbed woods, and layered textiles are specified not just for beauty but for how they support airflow, acoustics, and the rituals of daily living—from the first espresso to evening entertaining.

Where We Work

Projects stretch across the United States—from chef-driven restaurants in Santa Fe to luxury residences, real-estate developments, and commercial work along both coasts. Each scope leverages LEED-informed guidelines, advanced building technologies, and remote coordination to honor the surrounding architecture.

Mattéo Bitetti, Founder

Mattéo Bitetti is an accomplished interior designer and head of Mattéo Designs, a firm that specializes in providing comprehensive design services and project management for full-scale renovations and new construction projects in Santa Fe and throughout the country. He completed the Arc ID certificate program at UCLA and holds a master’s degree in Interior Architecture from Cal Poly Pomona. After living in Manhattan, Stockholm, and Los Angeles, Mattéo bought a home near the Santa Fe Opera in 2021 to be closer to the art and culture he loves.

In addition to his design work, Mattéo has an extensive background in the performing arts. He studied at the Mozarteum (International Summer Academy) in Salzburg, Austria; sang several years with the New York City Opera, and two years with the Folkoperan in Stockholm. His favorite composers are Puccini, Donizetti, Bizet, and other masters of the Bel Canto school. Mattéo is also a dedicated bodybuilder and former Mr. LA, bringing the same discipline and attention to detail from his athletic pursuits into every design project.
