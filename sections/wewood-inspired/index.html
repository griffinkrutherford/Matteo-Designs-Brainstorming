Who We Are

25+ years designing livable interiors.

Mattéo Designs has been in business for over 25 years, marrying traditional glamour with cutting-edge design for urban lifestyles nationwide. Our team delivers construction methodologies, FF&E selection, and project management that carry every concept through to completion.

What We Do

Full-service residential and commercial design including restaurants, boutique hotels, primary residences, and vacation homes. Services cover design development, space planning, lighting, material selection, FF&E, and installation oversight.

Our Philosophy

We believe interior design can change lives. Every space is artfully rendered yet deeply functional, delivering the powerful impact clients feel in daily routines.

Where We Work

Projects stretch across the United States—from Santa Fe adobe restorations to coastal condominiums—each tailored to the surrounding architecture and lifestyle.
