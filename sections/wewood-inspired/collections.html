Signatures

Limited collections rooted in Vanderhoof craft.

The Vanderhoof residence served as a living lab for three core Mattéo Designs series, blending European carpentry sensibilities with the softness of Southwestern light.

Casa Sol

Travertine plinths, limewashed walls, and arched millwork built to honor morning light. Featured rooms: Vanderhoof kitchen + salon.

Porto Line

Tambour doors, curved cabinetry, and grooved oak referencing Portuguese studios. Ideal for dining rooms and intimate libraries.

Desert Archivist

Modular shelving and bench systems fabricated with exposed joinery for art collectors and boutique hospitality lounges.
