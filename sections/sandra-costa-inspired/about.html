About Mattéo

Santa Fe interior designer focused on sustainable design.

Mattéo Designs specializes in creating interiors that reflect the unique character of Northern New Mexico. From historic adobe homes to contemporary spaces, our work honors the region's architectural beauty while meeting modern standards for sustainability and comfort.

Design Leadership

Since 2011, we've been transforming homes and commercial spaces through thoughtful design that combines beauty with function. Residential design involves psychology and an understanding of human behavior—we create custom homes, bathrooms, and kitchens that reflect your personal preferences and enhance how you live.

As a LEED certified studio, we integrate the latest building technologies and sustainable materials to create healthier, greener spaces that support modern living in Santa Fe.
