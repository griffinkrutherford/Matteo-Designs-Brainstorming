About Mattéo

Global studio, Santa Fe roots.

Mattéo Designs mirrors the ambition of Sandra Costa Design Group—delivering bespoke environments in Beverly Hills, Paris, Hong Kong, and Santa Fe while remaining grounded in the Vanderhoof residence craft.

Design Leadership

Women-led and LEED accredited since 2011. Our team directs architecture collaborations, furniture fabrication, and styling with sustainable sourcing.

The Vanderhoof family home epitomizes our process: layered cabinetry, gallery lighting, and sculptural bathrooms that inspired this alternate home experience.

25+Years combined leadership in architecture + interiors.
