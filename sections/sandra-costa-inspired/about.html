About Mattéo

25+ years of transformative interior design.

Mattéo Designs has been in business for over 25 years with interiors suited to modern lifestyles. We marry traditional glamour with cutting-edge design across the United States, bringing every client vision to life from concept to completion.

Design Leadership

We focus on construction methodologies that balance traditional glamour with modern efficiencies. Residential design involves psychology and an understanding of human behavior—we create custom homes, bathrooms, and kitchens that reflect your personal preferences and enhance how you live.

As a LEED certified firm, we integrate the latest building technologies, sustainable materials, and project management oversight to create healthier, greener spaces in Santa Fe and beyond.

Mattéo Bitetti

Originally from Los Angeles, Mattéo now leads the Santa Fe team, merging traditional glamour with cutting-edge construction methodologies. His on-site leadership keeps artisans, architects, and clients aligned from schematic design through final styling.

Who We Are

Mattéo Designs has been serving clients for over two decades with interiors suited to the urban lifestyle and modern aesthetics of today. Our portfolio spans historic adobe residences, boutique hotels, and coastal escapes—all unified by thoughtful detailing.

What We Do

We work in both residential and commercial design. Services include design development, space planning, lighting design, material selection, FF&E specification, and full project management for restaurants, boutique hotels, primary residences, and vacation homes.

Our Philosophy

We believe interior design can change lives. Every project prioritizes functional solutions paired with artfully rendered spaces, creating interiors that transform the way clients experience their daily routines.

Final styling, quality review, and detailed walkthrough to ensure every element meets project specifications.
