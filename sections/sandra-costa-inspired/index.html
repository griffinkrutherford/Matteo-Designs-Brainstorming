About Mattéo

Global studio, Santa Fe roots.

Mattéo Designs is a full-service architectural and interior design firm producing high-touch projects in Santa Fe, Beverly Hills, Paris, and Hong Kong. Since 2011, our practice has delivered mid-to-large scale remodels, new builds, custom furnishings, and curated art programs.
