Frequently Asked Questions

How much does interior design cost in Santa Fe?

Project costs vary based on scope and complexity. Typical residential projects range from $15,000 to $75,000+. We offer flexible packages to accommodate various budgets and project requirements.

Do you work on historic adobe homes?

Yes. Our firm has extensive experience with Santa Fe's historic properties, including comprehensive knowledge of preservation requirements and appropriate design approaches for adobe architecture.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by scope. A kitchen remodel typically takes 3-4 months from design to completion. Whole home renovations may take 6-12 months.

What is LEED certification?

LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) certification recognizes sustainable building practices. While not required, LEED principles can reduce energy costs and environmental impact.
