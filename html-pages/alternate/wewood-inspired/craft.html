Crafted by Hand

Slow furniture, informed by the Vanderhoof build.

Each commission follows an atelier-style cadence refined during the Vanderhoof residence. These stages keep artisan teams, architects, and clients aligned.

Phase 01

Design Study

Immersive workshops with material boards, VR walkthroughs, and archival research to define scale.

Phase 02

Wood Selection

Visiting trusted mills to hand-pick FSC oak, walnut, and ash with the right tone + grain.

Phase 03

Prototyping

3D printed joints & full-scale mockups ensure tambour curves and door reveals operate smoothly.

Phase 04

Detailing

Final finish schedule, brass patina testing, and installation choreography.

Material studies from Vanderhoof.

From the limestone primary bath to the dual-island kitchen, every finish was tested under Santa Fe light. We mapped the color drift throughout the day and tuned pigments accordingly.
