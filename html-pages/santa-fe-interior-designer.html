Frequently Asked Questions

How much does interior design cost in Santa Fe?

Project costs vary based on scope and complexity. Typical residential projects range from $15,000 to $75,000+. I offer flexible packages starting with consultations to fit various budgets. View our packages ‚Üí

Do you work on historic adobe homes?

Absolutely! I have extensive experience with Santa Fe's historic properties and understand the unique considerations required for adobe homes, including preservation requirements and appropriate design approaches.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by project scope. A kitchen remodel typically takes 3-4 months from design to completion. Whole home renovations may take 6-12 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

What is LEED certification and do I need it?

LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) certification recognizes sustainable building practices. While not required, LEED principles can reduce energy costs and environmental impact. Learn more about sustainable design ‚Üí
