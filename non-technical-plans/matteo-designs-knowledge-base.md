# Mattéo Designs Knowledge Base

Comprehensive reference for team members, partners, and collaborators who need a quick understanding of Mattéo Designs, the firm’s offerings, and the ideal client profile.

## 1. Brand Snapshot
- **Studios & Style**: Santa Fe–based, women-led, LEED-accredited interior design practice with European-influenced craftsmanship (Wewood-inspired alternate) and global luxury sensibility (Sandra Costa–inspired alternate).
- **Core Values**: Natural materials, sustainable sourcing, bespoke detailing, and deeply collaborative partnerships with architects, builders, and artisans.
- **Service Regions**: Santa Fe roots with active projects across Beverly Hills, Bel Air, Santa Monica, Hong Kong, Paris, and boutique destinations worldwide.

## 2. Primary Service Pillars

### Residential Design
- **New Builds**: Collaborate with architects/contractors from ground-up concept to completion, establishing unique design language per client.
- **Full Home Remodels & Refreshes**: Mood boards, space planning, floor plans, elevations, renderings, and procurement for cohesive transformations.
- **Kitchens & Bathrooms**: Gut renovations, smart-home integrations, technical updates, bespoke cabinetry, and luxury finishes.
- **Furniture, Fixtures, Equipment (FF&E)**: Curated sourcing or custom fabrication for every space, including bespoke millwork and lighting.
- **Interior/Exterior Integration**: Outdoor rooms, patios, and landscape-aligned palettes mirroring interior warmth.
- **Home Staging & Styling**: Showcase-ready layouts, art placement, textiles, and accessory styling.

### Commercial & Hospitality Design
- **Brand-Aligned Interiors**: Concepting spaces that express brand identity for restaurants, lounges, workplace, and retail environments.
- **Code-Compliant Documentation**: Construction drawings, specifications, and permitting packages meeting local regulations.
- **Budgeting & Project Controls**: Detailed cost planning, procurement tracking, and contractor coordination.
- **Project Management**: Timeline oversight, multi-vendor coordination, and issue resolution to maintain build-out efficiency.
- **High-Volume Functionality**: Solutions that balance aesthetics with traffic flow, durability, and operational needs.

### Specialized Expertise
- **LEED & Sustainability**: Emphasis on eco-friendly materials, low-VOC finishes, local sourcing (~92% within 500 miles), and energy-conscious planning.
- **Custom Furniture Editions**: Limited runs and one-of-one collector pieces inspired by the Vanderhoof residence prototypes.
- **Restoration & Adaptive Reuse**: Historical theaters, mansions, and cultural venues with archival research and preservation strategies.

## 3. Process Highlights
1. **Discovery & Questionnaire**: Website questionnaires (studio/contact pages) gather project type, timeline, investment range, and style priorities.
2. **Design Study**: Workshops, mood boards, VR walkthroughs, and archival references to define concept direction.
3. **Material Selection**: Visits to mills, pigment library tests, and finish sampling (documented during Vanderhoof project).
4. **Prototyping & Technical Planning**: Mockups, tambour/joinery prototypes, smart-home coordination, and permit-ready drawings.
5. **Fabrication & Installation**: Artisans (36 per season) craft custom pieces; project management ensures on-time delivery.
6. **Styling & Handover**: Final staging, art curation, and documentation for maintenance/operations.

## 4. Ideal Client Profile (ICP)
- **Residential**: Homeowners or developers seeking full-service involvement for new builds or comprehensive remodels, typically investing six figures or more. Value bespoke cabinetry, natural textures, and integrated indoor/outdoor living.
- **Commercial/Hospitality**: Restaurant groups, boutique hotels, and luxury retailers aiming to amplify brand identity and customer experience. Expect collaboration on budgets, compliance, and high-volume functionality.
- **Global, Design-Literate Decision Makers**: Comfortable with multi-phase timelines, appreciate artisan craftsmanship, and desire measurable ROI (brand elevation, property value, guest experience).
